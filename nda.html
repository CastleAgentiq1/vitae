1. Background

Innovation Capital Team LLP is acting as exclusive transaction advisor to Vitae Investments Ltd (the "Disclosing Party") in connection with the proposed sale of a portfolio of social housing assets (the "Transaction"). The Disclosing Party is willing to share certain confidential information with the person or entity named below (the "Receiving Party") solely to enable the Receiving Party to evaluate whether to proceed with the Transaction.

2. Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means all information disclosed by the Disclosing Party or its advisors in connection with the Transaction, including but not limited to: financial data, property information, lease agreements, occupancy details, tenant arrangements, planning consents, valuations, business plans, and the fact that the Transaction is taking place.

Confidential Information does not include information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available other than through a breach of this Agreement; (b) was already known to the Receiving Party before disclosure; or (c) is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of the Confidential Information.

3. Obligations of the Receiving Party

The Receiving Party agrees to: (a) keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential; (b) use it only for the purpose of evaluating the Transaction; (c) not disclose it to any third party without prior written consent, except to its own professional advisors who are bound by equivalent obligations of confidentiality; and (d) apply at least the same degree of care it applies to its own confidential information.

4. No Obligation to Proceed

Nothing in this Agreement obliges either party to proceed with the Transaction or to enter into any further agreement. The Disclosing Party reserves the right to withdraw from discussions at any time without liability.

5. Return of Information

Upon written request by the Disclosing Party, the Receiving Party shall promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information and any copies thereof, and confirm in writing that it has done so.

6. Duration

The obligations in this Agreement shall apply for a period of two (2) years from the date of signing, regardless of whether the Transaction proceeds.

7. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of England and Wales. Any disputes arising under it shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of England and Wales.

8. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties regarding confidentiality in connection with the Transaction and supersedes any prior understandings on the same subject matter.
