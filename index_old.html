Transaction Structure

A Clean, Passive-Yield Acquisition

From the owner's perspective, income is designed to be clean and consistent — one lease arrangement, no voids management, no contractor relationships, no local authority admin.

🏢

Sale + Leaseback

Buyer acquires the freehold portfolio. Castle Accommodation remains as operating tenant — assets transfer with income continuity and day-to-day management already in place.

📋

FRI Lease (Full Repairing & Insuring)

Castle Accommodation is responsible for all repairs, upkeep and insurance obligations as set out in the lease — landlord involvement reduced to a minimum.

💷

All Operating Costs Covered

Council tax, utilities, day-to-day maintenance, compliance works, and ongoing management — all covered by the tenant. Investor protected from cost inflation and operational volatility.

🏛

Local Authority Relationships Intact

Existing LA procurement relationships and placement demand remain under the same operator, reducing transition risk and supporting stable occupancy throughout the lease term.

📊

Reporting & Inspection Rights

Owner retains asset ownership and receives rent. Agreed reporting and inspection rights allow buyers to monitor performance and asset care without day-to-day involvement.

⚡

Flexible Deal Structure

Purchase of 100% issued share capital of Vitae Investments Ltd, or vendors would consider a sale of assets in a single transaction — whichever suits the buyer's structure.

The Investment Case

Social Housing: A Structurally Supported Asset Class

Demand is not cyclical, discretionary, or sentiment-driven — it is anchored in long-term demographic trends, chronic housing undersupply, and statutory obligations placed on local authorities. In effect, the end customer is not an individual household, but the public sector itself.

Defensive Income

Rental flows are supported directly or indirectly by local authorities, government-backed funding, or regulated care frameworks. This significantly reduces credit risk versus conventional residential or commercial tenants and materially lowers volatility through economic downturns.

Inflation Resilience

Rental frameworks, funding structures, and long-term agreements often incorporate indexation or periodic review mechanisms that protect real returns over time. Combined with long average tenancy durations and low void rates, this results in predictable, compounding cash flows.

Genuine ESG Alignment

Capital deployed here directly increases the supply and quality of housing for vulnerable individuals, reduces pressure on temporary accommodation and emergency services, and supports local authorities in meeting their statutory duties. Social value is measurable and inseparable from financial performance.

ESG & Social Responsibility

Impact Embedded in Every Asset

Our ESG approach is practical and measurable: safer homes, compliant refurbishment, and professional management — with social responsibility embedded in day-to-day decisions, not bolted on.

♿

Disability Adaptations

A significant share of the portfolio is adapted for residents with mobility needs — wider doorways, step-free access, adapted bathrooms and kitchens, and modern assistive features. This forms part of a long-term strategy to create safe, inclusive homes for everyone.

☀️

Solar Power & Energy Efficiency

Solar panels are installed across multiple assets to improve energy efficiency and reduce operating costs. Solar is increasingly integrated into refurbishment programmes and paired with sensor-controlled lighting and power management to maximise savings.

🏠

Insulation & Building Fabric

Walls, roofs, and windows are upgraded across the portfolio to reduce heat loss and lower ongoing energy costs. This work modernises older properties and delivers a healthier, more consistent indoor climate for residents year-round.
